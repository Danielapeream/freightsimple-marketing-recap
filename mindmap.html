↳ How this map gets used
Two purposes. First, every new channel idea or tactical proposal gets placed somewhere on this map before work starts: which pillar, which stage, who owns it, what KPI it moves. Second, the parked list keeps the broader vision visible without committing capacity. Channels move from parked to active when (a) the conversion math justifies it, (b) capacity allows it, or (c) a Q3/Q4 planning session promotes them deliberately. Nothing on this map runs on autopilot.
